Transmission in Dielectrics

This visualisation aims to explain a somewhat suprising property of waves incident on a dielectric boundary. More specifically, it will show that for any non-unity reflection coefficient, all of the energy of the incident wave can be made to transmit through the dielectric.

To understand this, consider a wave normally incident on a dielectric of thickness $t$. Recall that a wave can both transmit through and be reflected by a dielectric boundary. Here we label the reflection coefficient and transmission coefficient as $r$ and $t$ respectively.
